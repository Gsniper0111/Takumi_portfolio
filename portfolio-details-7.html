CyOp token, also known as CyOp Protocol, is a token based on the Ethereum blockchain. It is associated with a unique platform that allows Cyberpunks to invest, empower, and fund in disruptive ways. The CyOp Corporation, at the heart of the protocol, utilizes blockchain technology and artificial intelligence to hack the system and bring about revolutionary changes.

Key Features of CyOp Token:

  • 1. Voting Mechanism: CyOp tokens can be used to vote for any token on the Ethereum chain to be disrupted. This voting process involves locking the CyOp tokens for a specific period, resulting in a deflationary effect.
  • 2. Disruptive Market Buy Order: Once enough energy has been accumulated through voting, the CyOp Protocol executes a disruptive market buy order using 90% of the CyOp fund. This order targets the winning token, leading to potential market disruption.
  • 3. Token Burning: All the tokens that are bought during the disruptive market buy order are then "sucked into the resulting void" and burned forever. This burning mechanism helps reduce the supply of the targeted token.
  • 4. Matrix Glitch and Rewards: One holder who voted for the winning token experiences a matrix glitch in their favor and is awarded 10% of the CyOp fund. This incentivizes participation and engagement within the CyOp ecosystem.
